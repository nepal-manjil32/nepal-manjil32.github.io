About

I’m Manjil Nepal, a third-year B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering student at SRM University-AP, India, specializing in Deep Learning (DL) and Federated Learning (FL). My research primarily focuses on applying DL and FL in medical applications, exploring privacy-preserving techniques for decentralized healthcare AI.

I enjoy replicating machine learning research papers. Recently, I have started exploring Vision Transformers (ViTs) to understand their potential in modern computer vision tasks, alongside studying LLM fine-tuning techniques and their real-world applications. I work with PyTorch for Deep Learning and the Flower Framework for Federated Learning, constantly experimenting with new techniques to enhance model efficiency.

Beyond my technical interests, I have a strong passion for geopolitics, history, and international relations, continuously learning about global affairs and their impact on technology and society.
